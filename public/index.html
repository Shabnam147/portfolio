Case Studies

🏋️
Fitness · Mumbai
Iron Peak Gym
⚠ Problem

IRONPEAK had no online presence. Potential members had no way to discover the gym, explore membership plans, or contact the team — all leads came through word-of-mouth only, severely limiting growth.

⚙ Solution

Designed and built a high-energy, dark-aesthetic website with bold typography and strong calls-to-action. Included a full program showcase, coach profiles, tiered pricing with monthly/annual toggle, and a direct WhatsApp contact integration.

✓ Result

The gym now has a professional digital home that communicates its brand clearly. Members can find plans, explore programs, and enquire instantly.

📱 Fully Responsive 🚀 Live & Running

👗
E-Commerce · Fashion
Filia Fashion Store
⚠ Problem

A clothing brand was relying solely on Instagram DMs for orders. They had no structured way to showcase their full catalogue, no cart system, and no way to handle festive season demand at scale.

⚙ Solution

Built a premium e-commerce style website with multi-category navigation (Men, Women, Kids), a wishlist and shopping bag system, festive & bridal collection highlights, and promotional discount banners — all with a high-end editorial aesthetic.

✓ Result

Filia now has a polished storefront that converts casual browsers into buyers, with a clear path from discovery to checkout — no more lost orders in DMs.
